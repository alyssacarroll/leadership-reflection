About me

This project reflects on my international service experience during my time at Christopher Newport University and how it shaped my development as a leader and global citizen. Through immersion in a new environment, I was challenged to step outside of my comfort zone and engage with perspectives different from my own.

My experience emphasized the importance of understanding others, adapting to unfamiliar situations, and leading through service. These lessons not only influenced my leadership style but also continue to shape how I approach my future career and role in a global society.

Key Areas of Growth

  • Other Perspectives

    Through my international experience, I developed a deeper understanding of diverse perspectives by engaging with individuals from different cultural and social backgrounds. This challenged my assumptions and strengthened my ability to listen and adapt.

  • Resiliency

    Navigating unfamiliar environments required adaptability and problem-solving. I learned how to respond to challenges in real time, becoming more confident in my ability to handle uncertainty and overcome obstacles.

  • Transformational Leadership

    This experience reflects transformational leadership through personal growth and mindset change. I was inspired to think beyond myself and consider how leadership can create meaningful and lasting impact.

  • Servant Leadership

    Serving others was central to my experience. I learned that effective leadership begins with prioritizing the needs of others, fostering empathy, and contributing to a greater purpose.

Key Experiences

  • Cultural Immersion

    Immersing myself in a new culture pushed me to step outside of my comfort zone and adapt quickly. This experience strengthened my ability to navigate unfamiliar environments and appreciate different ways of life.
